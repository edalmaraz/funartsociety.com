About the society

Fun Art Society is an independent umbrella studio. Each of our ten houses does one thing and does it with unreasonable care — whether that's forging a sprite sheet, re-setting a hundred-year-old novel, or composing an eight-bit earworm. We keep our batches small, our prices fair, and our standards unfashionably high.

Everything is made digitally, delivered instantly, and sold through Gumroad — simple checkout, instant downloads, no account required.
