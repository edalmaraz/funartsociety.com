About the society

Fun Art Society is an independent umbrella studio. Each brand under our roof does one thing and does it with care — whether that's re-setting a hundred-year-old novel or composing an eight-bit earworm. Everything is made digitally, delivered digitally, and priced to be an easy yes.

Our shops run on Gumroad: simple checkout, instant downloads, no account required.
